How to play

  • Press the ← → arrow keys to move the shape sideways
  • Press the ↓ arrow key to accelerate the shape downwards
  • Press the ↑ arrow key to rotate the shape clockwise
  • Press the z key to rotate the shape anti-clockwise
  • Press the spacebar to rapidly drop the shape
  • To start a new game, please refresh the page
